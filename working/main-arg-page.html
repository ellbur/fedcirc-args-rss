Listen to Oral Arguments

Home ­­» Oral Argument ­­» Listen to Oral Arguments

The Federal Circuit is providing media and public access to the audio stream of each panel scheduled for argument during the court’s monthly sessions.  This access is part of the Federal Circuit’s response to the COVID-19 pandemic and the continued limitation on public access to the National Courts Building.  See Administrative Order 2021-10 (June 22, 2021) (pdf).

The streaming audio feeds begin approximately 15 minutes before the start of the scheduled argument in each courtroom.  Recordings of all arguments will be posted on the court’s website after the conclusion of arguments each day.
